At Stockholm Social Wine Club, we are more than just a club; we are a community of passionate individuals brought together by a shared love for the finer things in life. Whether you're a seasoned connoisseur or just beginning your adventure into the realm of wines, our club is your passport to an extraordinary vinous experience.

About Us


Embark on a worldwide exploration of flavors without leaving the comfort of your home. Each month, our curated selection transports you to renowned vineyards, introducing you to unique varietals, exceptional vintages, and the stories behind the labels. From the sun-kissed vineyards of Napa Valley to the rolling hills of Tuscany, our carefully chosen wines are a celebration of diversity and craftsmanship.



Monthly subscription includes:


  • Curated Wine Selection:

    Each month, members receive a carefully curated selection of wines. These could be chosen based on themes, regions, or unique varietals, allowing members to explore different aspects of the world of wine.

  • Tasting Notes and Information:

    Provide detailed tasting notes and information about each wine in the monthly selection. Include background stories about the vineyards, winemakers, and the unique characteristics of the wines. This adds an educational element to the experience.

  • Food Pairing Recommendations:

    Enhance the tasting experience by suggesting food pairings for each wine. Whether it's a delightful cheese, a sumptuous dish, or decadent chocolates, pairing recommendations can elevate the overall enjoyment of the wines.

  • Exclusive Discounts:

    Offer members exclusive discounts on additional wine purchases. This could be a perk for being part of the club, encouraging members to explore and expand their personal wine collections.

  • Access to Virtual Tastings:

    Host virtual tastings or webinars featuring wine experts, sommeliers, or even representatives from the wineries themselves. This interactive experience allows members to learn more about the wines they receive and connect with fellow wine enthusiasts.

  • Members-Only Events:

    Organize special events exclusively for club members. This could include wine tours, vineyard visits, or local tastings. Creating opportunities for members to meet and share their passion for wine enhances the sense of community within the club.
